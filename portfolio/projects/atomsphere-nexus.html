AtomSphere Nexus — Goal Intelligence & Performance Tracking SaaS

A multi-role SaaS platform that streamlines employee performance management, goal tracking, project monitoring, KPI evaluation, and review cycles through one centralized system — with a different dashboard for every role that touches it.

01 Overview

Performance management inside most companies lives across scattered spreadsheets, one-off review documents, and disconnected chat threads — which makes it hard for a manager to see a team's real progress, and harder still for leadership to roll that up into anything reliable. AtomSphere Nexus replaces that patchwork with a single SaaS platform where goals, KPIs, and review cycles all live in one place, visible to the right people at the right level of detail.

02 The problem

Three different people need three different views of the same underlying data: an employee needs to see and update their own goals; a manager needs a team-wide view to run reviews and evaluate KPIs fairly; an admin needs organization-wide visibility and reporting. Building that as one undifferentiated dashboard either overwhelms employees with controls they don't need, or strips managers of the oversight they do. AtomSphere Nexus was built to give each role exactly what it needs — no more, no less — without duplicating the underlying data model three times.

03 Architecture

01
Role-based dashboards
Separate, purpose-built views for Employees, Managers, and Admins, built on a shared data model and gated by role-based access control.
02
Goal & KPI engine
Structured goal tracking and KPI evaluation that feeds directly into scheduled review cycles instead of living in a separate document.
03
Real-time sync
Built on Supabase, so a goal update or review comment reflects across every relevant dashboard immediately, not after a page refresh.
04
Automated reporting
One-click generation of PDF and Excel reports for review cycles and organizational analytics, removing manual report assembly.

The front end is built in React.js, with Supabase handling authentication, real-time data sync, and storage on top of a relational SQL schema, and a REST API layer connecting the dashboards to that data.

04 What it handles

  • Employee goal tracking with manager visibility
  • KPI evaluation tied directly to review cycles
  • Project monitoring across teams
  • Role-based dashboards for Employees, Managers, and Admins
  • Real-time synchronization across every connected dashboard
  • Automated PDF / Excel report generation for reviews and analytics
  • Secure, role-gated data access throughout
